Pop 'n Tanks is a tank combat game that strives for the dimunitive and the comic rather than the realistic. The players pilot a mini-tank across a 3D terrain, crushing barricades and shelling other tanks. Tanks may be customized by using a variety of parts, and once configured to the player's preferences, the data may be saved to a Memory Card. 
 This is a tank battle game. The player who wins 2 rounds win the match. In order to win a match the player must empty the enemy's energy bar. 
 You'll get to battle in all sort of arena with differentterrain ranging from lush forest, down town area, to spooky caves. 
 Pop'n Tanks allows you to take control one of many SD tanks into 1 on 1 battle against an opponent. One can play in story mode to face all enenmies in progrssive order or have a quick battle or use your saved tanks in custom battle against a friend. The game has different game modes: 
 - TANK WORLD: In this mode you get to create your own tank (based on the default tanks) to customise and compete in the tank world's tank competition. 
 - STORY MODE: In which the player choose to play as 1 of eight chracters in a quest to beat all the others. The game characters are: Puel, Bit-Man, Puela, Vokkle, Domina & Hekkle, Ferina, Gunt & Date 
 - QUICK BATTLE (Play against an oponent in a split screen mode) 
 - CUSTOM BATTLE (2 players only. You get to battle with your customised tank from TANK 
WORLD.
